Our Story

Forged in Shadow

A legacy of uncompromising performance, visionary design, and the relentless pursuit of automotive perfection.

The Beginning

Where It All Started

Obsidian was born from a single obsession: to create the most visceral driving experience on the planet. Founded in 2014, our team of visionary engineers and designers set out to challenge every convention of the automotive world.

The name "Obsidian" — volcanic glass formed under extreme pressure — perfectly captures our philosophy. Beauty born from intensity. Strength from transformation. Darkness as the ultimate canvas for light.

Heritage

Our Journey

2014

The Founding

Obsidian Motors is established with a vision to redefine luxury performance. A small team of 12 engineers begins work on the first prototype.

2016

Shadow Unveiled

The Obsidian Shadow, our inaugural grand tourer, debuts at the Geneva Motor Show to critical acclaim and overwhelming demand.

2018

Phantom Dominance

The Phantom hypercar launches, breaking three production car records in its first year. Obsidian enters the world stage.

2021

Global Expansion

Obsidian opens showrooms across 34 countries. The Eclipse and Vortex join the lineup, each pushing boundaries further.

2026

The Electric Future

The Spectre, our first all-electric hypercar, is announced. 900 horsepower. Zero emissions. The next chapter begins.

Design Language

Darkness is Our Canvas

Our design philosophy starts with darkness. Where others add, we subtract. Where others decorate, we sculpt. Every line on an Obsidian vehicle exists for a reason — to channel air, command attention, or express power.

We draw inspiration from volcanic obsidian glass: surfaces that are simultaneously dark and reflective, sharp yet smooth, ancient yet timeless. This duality drives every design decision we make.
